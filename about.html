How to play:

The goal is to reach the highest possible point jumping in the various platform. Click <- left or -> right to move your fox, the jump is executed from a collision with a platform.

About us:

This game is performed for the exam of Interactive Graphics by Andrea Frabotta and Flavio Scaccia.

Libraries:

Three.js
Physijs.js - Used to perform collisions
Tween.js - Used to create animations of the fox
GLTFLoader.js - Used to load the hierarchical model of the fox
